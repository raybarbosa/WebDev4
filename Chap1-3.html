This is a quick recipe to scramble eggs

First you need the following

  • Eggs
  • Fork or whisk
  • bowl to stir eggs in
  • warm pan
  • clean bowl for finished eggs

Here are the steps

  1. Warm up the pan
  2. Crack eggs in the bowl
  3. Using fork or whisk, stir up the eggs
  4. Pour eggs into warm pan
  5. Cook eggs until they settle
  6. Pour cooked eggs into clean bowl
